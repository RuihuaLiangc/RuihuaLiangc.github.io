Key Features of Sarco

  • Autonomy: Sarco can be operated by the individual seeking euthanasia alone, ensuring complete autonomy.
  • No Pain:Sarco offers a non-invasive, painless way to die without the need for medical professionals.
  • Portable and Reusable: The base of the Sarco device is reusable, while the capsule is a 3D printed disposable product.
  • Environmentally Friendly: The capsules are biodegradable, reducing the impact on the environment.
